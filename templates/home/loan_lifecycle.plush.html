Loan Lifecycle Management

Understanding the complete journey of a loan from proposal to disbursement

1

Loan Proposal

Borrower submits loan application with required documents

  • Basic information collection
  • Document submission
  • Initial screening
2

Field Validation

Field validator verifies borrower details and documents

  • Document verification
  • Field visit
  • Validation report
3

Loan Approval

Loan gets approved based on validation results

  • Risk assessment
  • Credit scoring
  • Terms finalization
4

Investment Phase

Investors fund the approved loan

  • Investment allocation
  • Fund collection
  • Investment tracking
5

Disbursement

Loan amount is disbursed to borrower

  • Agreement signing
  • Fund transfer
  • Documentation
